1. Executive Summary

StudyTales is an AI-powered educational RPG that transforms studying into a progression-based gaming experience.

Players import study materials, complete AI-generated learning activities, earn rewards, collect cards, build teams, and progress through an ever-expanding ecosystem of educational content.

The core philosophy is:

Every minute spent studying should feel rewarding.

Unlike traditional flashcard applications, StudyTales combines:

* Adaptive Learning
* AI Content Generation
* Gacha Collection Systems
* RPG Team Building
* Character Interactions
* Long-Term Progression

into a single educational platform.
